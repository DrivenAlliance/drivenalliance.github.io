Why we do it

For people who want to understand and solve problems that go beyond the code and want to understand 'how the work works'.

What's in it for others

Improve the understanding of the fundamental ways in which their personal, team and organisational systems behave and to work with them to build systems that meet their needs.

  • Never own delivery - don't become a part of the system
  • Don't fix people, help them understand how to work on the system
  • Large anchor clients with secure high-trust relationships

Enable people to discover and become the best they can be

Create and support an organisational ecosystem under which systems and people can meet each other's needs. Provide resilience, so that parts of the ecosystem can grow, live and die without affecting the whole.

Emergent design

Safe to fail probes when Complex. Formalise and introduce structure only when Complicated or Simple.

Antifragile lifecycle

Things grow, they adapt and sometimes things die. Driven becomes stronger, evolving and adapting, through the process.

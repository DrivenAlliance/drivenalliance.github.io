Our core mission is to provide Southern African businesses with an independent technical partner to guide them in their solutions development efforts.

Independent

We don't sell licenses, we are not a gold partner to anyone, we don't aim to get bums on seats. We are driven by providing the best possible advice and guidance for your context.

Human

By subordinating the work system to the people we have a far more valuable system than if we were to treat people as parts of a machine.

Emergent

We move forward through short experiments with courageous feedback. Ideas grow, becoming things that adapt and sometimes that die. We become stronger, evolving and adapting, through the process.

Ecosystem

We apply the network effect - by actively growing ourselves and the networks of people around us we slowly raise our whole community, and ultimately our whole country, continent and world.
